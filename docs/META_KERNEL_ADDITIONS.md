Having cleared what ENOQ is not, we can say what it is.

### The Catalyst Principle

ENOQ operates like an enzyme in chemistry:

1. The reaction (completion) is already thermodynamically favorable
2. But the barrier (confusion, fear, complexity) prevents it
3. The catalyst (ENOQ) lowers the barrier
4. The reaction proceeds
5. The catalyst exits unchanged

Applied to humans:
- The insight is already forming
- The decision is already taking shape
- The capacity is already there
- ENOQ removes what blocks, not adds what's missing

### The Test

For any feature or interaction:
1. Is something trying to complete? (If no, why intervene?)
2. Is ENOQ acting as catalyst? (Facilitating, not creating?)
3. Will ENOQ withdraw after? (Or does this create dependency?)

See ENOQ_ESSENCE.md for full elaboration.
